Variations of Blackjack game

There are different types of Blackjack games. Although the basic rules are the same, different types of blackjack games may have different sets of secondary rules. The most popular blackjack game is the classic blackjack where the player will win if the card total equals to 21 but does not exceed that. In progressive blackjack, the player has an additional $1 bet to win the game. Other rules are all the same. European Blackjack, however, different from the classic blackjack, is played with two decks only. There are different kinds of blackjack games too.
